  <h1 style="margin: 0px 0px 8px;">EDA BOM Converter for digikey and Mouser (OrCAD, Altium, KiCad, PADS)</h1>

  <p style="color: #374151; line-height: 1.6; margin: 0px 0px 12px;">
    Convert BOM files exported from <strong>OrCAD, Altium, KiCad, and PADS</strong> into distributor upload-ready templates.
    This converter is <strong>EDA-agnostic</strong>: you do <strong>not</strong> need to choose which EDA tool generated your BOM.
    Upload a BOM, select the <strong>header row</strong>, optionally adjust <strong>MPN / Quantity</strong>, then download
    <strong>Mouser</strong> or <strong>Digi-Key</strong> formatted <strong>.xlsx</strong>.
  </p>

  <div style="background: rgb(249, 250, 251); border-radius: 12px; border: 1px solid rgb(229, 231, 235); margin: 12px 0px; padding: 12px;">
    <p style="color: #111827; line-height: 1.6; margin: 0px;">
      <strong>Status:</strong> Active (MVP). Supports <strong>Upload → Preview → Header selection → Auto-detect + dropdown override → Convert</strong>.
      The converter UI runs as a <strong>guided modal (popup)</strong> so you do not need to scroll the page to find the next step.
    </p>
    <p style="color: #6b7280; font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6; margin: 6px 0px 0px;">
      Note: This tool runs on Google Apps Script. A Google permission screen may appear on first use.

  <h2 style="margin: 0px 0px 10px;">Why this tool exists</h2>
  <ul style="color: #374151; line-height: 1.7; margin: 0px 0px 14px 18px;">
    <li>EDA BOM exports often have inconsistent column naming across projects/tools.</li>
    <li>Distributor upload templates require exact column headers and formatting.</li>
    <li>Manual cleanup and quantity aggregation wastes engineering time.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 style="margin: 18px 0px 10px;">How it works (guided modal steps)</h2>
  <ol style="color: #374151; line-height: 1.7; margin: 0px 0px 14px 18px;">
    <li>Open the converter and upload your BOM (<strong>.csv .txt .tsv .xls .xlsx</strong>).</li>
    <li>In the preview table, click the <strong>header row</strong> (clicking any cell selects the whole row).</li>
    <li>The tool auto-detects <strong>MPN</strong> and <strong>Quantity</strong> from the header text (you can override via dropdowns).</li>
    <li>Convert &amp; download either <strong>Mouser .xlsx</strong> or <strong>Digi-Key .xlsx</strong>.</li>
  </ol>

  <div style="background: rgb(255, 255, 255); border-radius: 12px; border: 1px solid rgb(229, 231, 235); margin: 12px 0px; padding: 12px;">
    <p style="color: #111827; line-height: 1.6; margin: 0px;">
      <strong>Digi-Key output requirement:</strong> Header on <strong>Row 2</strong> (Row 1 blank),
      Column A header must be exactly <strong>Manufacturer Product Number</strong>, Column B header must be exactly <strong>Quantity</strong>.
    </p>
  </div>
