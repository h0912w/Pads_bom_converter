  <h2 style="margin: 18px 0px 10px;">How to use (step-by-step)</h2>
  <ol style="color: #374151; line-height: 1.7; margin: 0px 0px 14px 18px;">
    <li>Upload your BOM file (CSV/TXT/TSV/XLS/XLSX).</li>
    <li>Check the preview table (the tool highlights likely header rows).</li>
    <li>Click <strong>one MPN</strong> cell (Manufacturer Part Number / Part Number / Mfr P/N).</li>
    <li>Click <strong>one Quantity</strong> cell (QTY / Quantity).</li>
    <li>Click <strong>Convert</strong> to download <strong>Mouser .xlsx</strong> or <strong>Digi-Key .xlsx</strong>.</li>
  </ol>
